Welcome to Sniper Shop

Bow Sports archery offers expert advice on all aspects of archery supplies. Based in Wolverhampton we have the largest stocked showroom in the midlands. Established since 1984, Bow Sports is a family run business stocking the widest selection of archery supplies, archery equipment and archery targets, so you should easily find what you are looking for in our online shop. Take the time to browse our products and discover the great deals that we have on Mathews, PSE, Martin, Easton and other top brand archery equipment. With 30 years in the archery business, not only supplying archers, we also specialise in supplying Leisure Centres, Hotels and Archery Clubs, if you're looking to purchase archery equipment, targets, bows and arrows then you've come to the right place. Our range of archery supplies makes us one of the best online archery specific shops in the UK, we work constantly to improve our services, and all feedback is greatly appreciated.
